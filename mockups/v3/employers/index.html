The Hiring Problem

Current AI hiring is broken.

You need people who can actually build with AI. But every signal you rely on today is unreliable, outdated, or gameable.

Resumes Lie

"Proficient in AI" is the new "Proficient in Excel." Anyone can list it. Nobody verifies it. You end up interviewing people who watched a tutorial.

Interviews Miss

A 45-minute behavioral interview cannot reveal if someone can actually prompt, iterate, debug, and ship with AI tools. You are testing presentation, not capability.

Certifications Decay

AI moves faster than any certification body. By the time someone earns a badge, the tools have changed. Static credentials cannot keep pace with a moving target.
